    Hi! My name is Riley and I love to read.

    I don't think you can have an about page without a background story and I think it's the best way to express how much I love to read. When I was growing up I spent a decent amount of my free time reading books.
    I loved books so much that when I got in trouble my mom would take away my reading privileges. Most nights after my bedtime you could find me reading a book by the door to my room, using the hallway light to be able to see the pages.
    As life got busier, I stopped reading so much. Recently, I rediscovered how much I LOVE to read. I set a goal to read 25 books in a year and blew that goal out of the water within the first month and a half.
    But here's the challenge: how can I find more books to read? That's where bibliobibuli comes in. Here you can share your reviews of books you've read and see what books your friends are reading.
